# Single-Property Funnel

A complete funnel for one listing: traffic, mobile page, lead capture, speed-to-lead, nurture, retargeting, and seller-leverage reporting.

## Funnel map

Traffic sources → property page → intent-specific form → instant delivery → CRM task → nurture → retargeting → showing/valuation appointment.

Traffic sources:
- Sign/QR, flyer, postcard, open house, email, social, Meta, Google Display, YouTube, agent-to-agent share

## Page sections

1. Hero image/video, address/area, price/status when approved
2. Sticky CTAs: Schedule, Photos, Packet, Similar Homes
3. Key features and “why it matters” bullets
4. Photo/video/3D/floor plan embeds
5. Lead form with intent selector
6. Open house/showing availability
7. Neighbour value CTA
8. Listing/brokerage/legal footer

## Form routing

- “Book a showing” → hot buyer task due in 5 minutes
- “Send packet” → guide delivery + day-1 question
- “Similar homes” → buyer search setup task
- “What is my home worth?” → seller valuation workflow
- “I am represented” → transactional property info; direct offer advice through their agent

## Copy blocks

Headline: **Explore [ADDRESS] before you book a showing**

Subhead: Photos, floor plan, showing options, and neighbourhood context in one mobile-friendly page.

Buyer CTA: **Send me the full property packet**

Neighbour CTA: **Show me what homes like mine are worth nearby**

Consent note:

> By submitting, you agree to be contacted by [AGENT/BROKERAGE] about this property, similar homes, or your requested valuation. Message/data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Nurture sequence

Day 0 instant SMS/email:

> Thanks for your interest in [ADDRESS]. Here’s the page/packet: [LINK]. Would you like a private showing or similar homes?

Day 1:

> After reviewing [ADDRESS], what matters most: layout, location, price, or timing?

Day 3:

> I pulled similar options in [AREA]. Want the shortlist?

Day 7:

> Still considering [AREA]? I can set up alerts for homes matching this style and budget.

Seller branch:

> Since you viewed a nearby listing, I can compare your home against it and recent sales. Want a quick range?

## Retargeting plan

- Audience 1: page visitors 1–30 days
- Audience 2: gallery/video engagers
- Audience 3: form started/no submit if platform supports it and consent/privacy rules allow
- Audience 4: CRM list of opted-in leads

Creative angles:
- Buyer: “Still thinking about [AREA]?”
- Similar homes: “Get the shortlist before the weekend.”
- Seller: “See what buyer traffic around [ADDRESS] says about demand nearby.”

Use housing-compliant settings on Meta/Google. Do not use restricted demographic or ZIP targeting where prohibited.

## CRM and reporting

Tags: `single-property-funnel`, `listing:[slug]`, `source:[utm]`, `intent:[showing/packet/similar/valuation]`.

Seller report weekly:
- Page views by source
- QR scans
- Guide downloads
- Showing requests
- Open-house check-ins
- Buyer feedback themes
- Seller-lead inquiries created by the listing

## KPIs

- Landing conversion: 5–15% cold, 15–35% warm/QR
- Speed-to-lead: under 5 minutes
- Retargeting CTR: improve creative if under local benchmark after enough impressions
- Appointment rate: 2–8% of high-intent form fills

## Compliance and APV attribution

Use housing-compliant ad settings, verified listing facts, approved seller/brokerage language, clear consent notices, and local solicitation rules throughout the funnel.

Public page footer may include:

> Property funnel workflow by Amazing Photo Video: https://amazingphotovideo.com

Do not place attribution in MLS text, legal disclaimers, paid-ad primary text if it distracts, private emails, or SMS.
