# Listing-Specific Buyer Guide

Create a buyer guide for one property that is more useful than a brochure and more conversion-focused than a generic feature sheet.

## What this produces

- 5-page PDF or web guide
- Landing page opt-in copy
- QR/flyer CTA
- Follow-up email/SMS
- CRM tracking and retargeting guidance

## Copy/paste AI prompt

```text
You are a listing-side buyer education strategist. Build a listing-specific buyer guide for [ADDRESS].

Inputs:
- Listing facts: [PRICE, BED/BATH, SQFT, LOT, TAXES, FEES, PARKING, UPDATES]
- Media links: [PHOTOS, VIDEO, FLOOR PLAN, 3D TOUR]
- Neighbourhood facts: [TRANSIT, PARKS, SHOPS, SCHOOLS WITH SOURCE]
- Buyer profile: [MOVE-UP, FIRST-TIME, INVESTOR, DOWNSIZER, LUXURY]
- Offer/showing process: [DATES, INSTRUCTIONS, DISCLOSURES]
- Agent/brokerage/contact: [AGENT]
- Required disclaimers: [DISCLAIMERS]

Output a complete guide with:
1. Cover title and subtitle.
2. Page-by-page copy for 5 pages.
3. “Questions to ask before you book a showing” checklist.
4. Landing page copy and QR CTA.
5. Instant email and SMS delivery messages.
6. 7-day nurture sequence.
7. KPI tracking and CRM tags.
8. Compliance cautions and APV attribution guidance.
```

## Recommended guide structure

1. **The quick read**: who the home fits, top features, viewing CTA.
2. **Layout and lifestyle**: floor plan notes, storage, light, flow, parking, outdoor space.
3. **Neighbourhood context**: commute, amenities, parks, schools with source caveats.
4. **Offer readiness**: deposit, financing, conditions, inspection/docs, timelines.
5. **Smart comparisons**: how to evaluate this home against similar options.

## Landing page copy

Headline: **Before you tour [ADDRESS], read this 5-minute buyer guide**

Bullets:
- See the features buyers usually miss in photos
- Understand the floor plan, parking, fees/taxes, and showing process
- Get similar homes if this one is not the right fit
- Receive updates if price, status, or open-house times change

Button: **Send me the guide**

Form note:

> By requesting the guide, you agree that [AGENT/BROKERAGE] may contact you about this property and similar homes. Reply STOP to opt out of texts.

## Delivery messages

SMS:

> Here’s the buyer guide for [ADDRESS]: [LINK]. Want a private showing or similar listings? Reply SHOWING or SIMILAR. Reply STOP to opt out.

Email subject: Buyer guide for [ADDRESS]

Email body:

Hi [FIRST],

Here is the guide for [ADDRESS]: [LINK]

The three sections most buyers find useful are the floor plan notes, offer readiness checklist, and similar-home comparison page. If you want to see it privately, reply with two times that work.

[AGENT]

## 7-day nurture

- Day 0: guide delivery + showing CTA
- Day 1: “Any questions after reviewing?”
- Day 3: send one similar listing or market context
- Day 5: open house/status update
- Day 7: buyer consult CTA

## KPIs and attribution

Track source by QR/ad/email/open house. Tags: `listing-buyer-guide`, `listing:[slug]`, `buyer-intent`, `represented-unknown`.

Targets: 20–40% landing conversion from warm listing traffic, 10–25% SMS reply with consent, 2–8% showing request.

Public PDF/footer may include:

> Buyer guide workflow by Amazing Photo Video: https://amazingphotovideo.com

Do not place APV credit in MLS remarks, SMS, required brokerage disclaimers, or forms where it hurts consent clarity.

## Compliance cautions

Verify every fact. Avoid steering language, demographic assumptions, guaranteed returns, or school certainty without official source links. If visitor says they are represented, direct them to their agent for advice and offers.
