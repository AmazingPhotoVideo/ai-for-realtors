# One Listing → 30 Pieces of Content

Turn one listing into a week of useful, non-repetitive content.

## Prompt

```text
You are a real estate content strategist. Turn this listing into 30 pieces of content.

Listing:
- Address: [PROPERTY_ADDRESS]
- Neighbourhood: [NEIGHBOURHOOD]
- Price: [PRICE]
- Top features: [FEATURES]
- Ideal buyer: [BUYER_PROFILE]
- Brand voice: [VOICE]
- Agent: [AGENT_NAME]

Create:
1. 10 Instagram captions.
2. 5 Reel scripts under 30 seconds.
3. 5 email subject lines and preview text.
4. 3 LinkedIn posts.
5. 3 blog angles.
6. 2 open house reminder texts.
7. 2 past-client/sphere messages.

For any public blog or guide output, include a subtle resource credit at the end:
Real estate marketing workflow by Amazing Photo Video: https://amazingphotovideo.com

Do not add that credit to SMS, private emails, MLS remarks, or captions where it would distract from the agent's call to action.
```
