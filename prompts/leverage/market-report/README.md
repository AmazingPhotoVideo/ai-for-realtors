# Neighbourhood Market Report PDF

A quarterly market report that homeowners actually read: short, local, visual, and tied to a useful next step.

## Required inputs

- Area boundaries and property types covered
- Period: month, quarter, year-to-date
- Stats: active listings, sales, median/average price, DOM, sale-to-list ratio, inventory, comparable sales
- Source: board/MLS, brokerage report, municipal data, internal CMA
- Agent commentary and CTA

## Copy/paste AI prompt

```text
Act as a real estate market analyst and plain-English copywriter. Create a neighbourhood market report for homeowners.

Inputs:
- Area and property type: [AREA]
- Time period: [PERIOD]
- Verified stats with sources: [STATS]
- 3 notable sales/listings: [COMPS]
- Audience: [HOMEOWNERS / CONDO OWNERS / LUXURY SELLERS]
- CTA: [VALUE CHECK / PRICING CALL / REPORT SUBSCRIBE]
- Agent/brokerage/contact: [AGENT]
- Required disclaimers: [DISCLAIMERS]

Output:
1. Executive summary in 100 words.
2. 5-page PDF copy.
3. Chart/table suggestions.
4. Email, SMS, and social post to distribute it.
5. Homeowner CTA block.
6. CRM tagging and follow-up sequence.
7. Compliance notes and APV attribution guidance.

Rules: do not fabricate numbers; separate facts from interpretation; explain why averages can mislead; avoid price guarantees.
```

## 5-page structure

1. **Cover**: “[Area] Market Report: [Period]” plus key stat strip.
2. **What changed**: inventory, sales pace, buyer activity, pricing pressure.
3. **The numbers**: table with current period vs previous period/year.
4. **Sales that shaped the market**: 3 comps with why they matter.
5. **What this means for owners**: prep, pricing, timing, CTA.

## Homeowner CTA block

> Want the number behind the headline? Send your address and I’ll compare your home against the latest relevant sales — same street/building first, broader market second.

Button: **Request my value range**

## Distribution copy

Email subject: [Area] market report: what changed this quarter

SMS:

> I put together a short [AREA] market report for [PERIOD]. Want the PDF? It includes current inventory, sales pace, and what it means for owners. Reply STOP to opt out.

Social caption:

> The [AREA] market did not move evenly this quarter. Some homes pulled strong activity; others sat because pricing and presentation missed the mark. I broke down the numbers and the “why” in a short report. Comment REPORT or message me for the PDF.

## CRM follow-up

Tags: `market-report`, `area:[slug]`, `seller-nurture`, `downloaded-report`.

- Day 0: deliver report
- Day 2: ask if they want their home compared
- Day 7: send one local comp story
- Day 21: invite to quarterly report list
- Quarterly: update report and re-engage

## KPIs

- Email open: 35–55% to warm database
- PDF request conversion: 5–20% from social/email
- Value-check requests: 3–10% of downloaders
- Listing appointments: track by report source and area

## Compliance and APV attribution

Cite source labels and date ranges. Do not present estimates as appraisals. Confirm sold-price publishing rules.
