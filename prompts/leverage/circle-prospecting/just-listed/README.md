# Just-Listed Circle Prospecting Kit

Turn a new listing into neighbour conversations without sounding like a robocall. Use this for the 100 closest doors, condo stack, or farm segment around the property.

## When to use

- Day 0–3 after launch, once MLS/public marketing is live and seller approval is documented.
- Before open houses, broker tours, or weekend showing blocks.
- When you need buyer leads, seller curiosity, and local proof from one listing.

## Required inputs

- Agent name, brokerage, phone, email, booking link
- Listing address or neighbourhood, property type, price range, launch date
- 3 factual differentiators: lot, renovation, schools, views, transit, parking, suite potential, amenities
- Open house/showing window and landing page/QR URL
- Compliance requirements from brokerage/board

## Field-ready scripts

### 30-second call

> Hi, is this [NAME]? It’s [AGENT] with [BROKERAGE]. I’m calling because we just listed a home near you at [ADDRESS/AREA], and neighbours usually want to know what is happening before the weekend traffic starts. Quick question: would it be helpful if I sent you the photo page and open house details, or are you mostly curious what this means for values on your street?

If interested:

> Perfect. I’ll send the link. If you want, I can also give you a quick range for your place based on the latest nearby sales — no appointment needed unless you ask for one.

If not interested:

> Totally fair. I’ll leave you be. If you see extra cars this weekend, that’s what it is. Have a good day.

### Door-knock

> Hi, I’m [AGENT]. I’m not here to pressure you — I listed [ADDRESS/AREA], and I wanted the closest neighbours to have the details before the general public starts showing up. Here’s the QR page with photos, open house times, and a quick value-check option if you’re curious what a sale like this means for your home.

### Voicemail

> Hi [NAME], it’s [AGENT] with [BROKERAGE]. We just brought [ADDRESS/AREA] to market and I’m updating nearby owners so nobody is surprised by weekend activity. I can text the photo page and open house details if useful. My number is [PHONE].

### SMS with consent context

> Hi [NAME], it’s [AGENT] with [BROKERAGE]. We just listed [ADDRESS/AREA]. Want the photo page/open house details, or a quick read on what this could mean for nearby values? Reply STOP to opt out.

### Email

Subject: New listing near [STREET/BUILDING]

Hi [NAME],

I’m sharing this with the closest neighbours first: [ADDRESS/AREA] is now live.

Why it matters locally:
- [FACT 1]
- [FACT 2]
- [FACT 3]

Here is the property page: [URL]

If you are curious what this listing means for your home’s value, reply with your address and I’ll send a quick, no-pressure range using the latest comparable sales.

[AGENT]

## CRM flow

- Source: `listing-leverage-just-listed`
- Tags: `neighbour`, `listing:[slug]`, `qr-scan` or `call-answer`, `seller-curious`, `buyer-curious`
- Pipeline stages: New → Sent listing link → Asked value question → Appointment offered → Appointment booked → Long-term nurture
- Day 0: call/door knock + send link
- Day 1: open house reminder to opted-in contacts
- Day 3: “traffic/feedback” update
- Day 7: soft value-check CTA

## KPIs

- 100 attempts → 20–35 conversations
- 10–20 landing page visits from offline outreach
- 3–8 value-check requests or buyer guide downloads
- 1–3 listing appointment opportunities per strong listing/farm fit

## Compliance cautions

- Respect Do Not Call, CASL/TCPA, A2P 10DLC, brokerage policies, and local board rules.
- Do not call/text without a lawful basis or consent where required.
- Do not use protected-class language or imply a neighbourhood is for a specific demographic.
- Verify all listing facts, school boundaries, taxes, square footage, and availability before publishing.
