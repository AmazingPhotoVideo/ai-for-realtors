# One Listing → 30 Pieces of Content

Turn one listing into a week of useful, non-repetitive content across social, email, blog, SMS, retargeting, and seller proof.

## Input checklist

- Listing facts, media links, approved features, showing/open-house details
- Neighbourhood context and source links
- Ideal audience: buyer, neighbour, seller, investor, luxury, downsizer
- Brand voice and forbidden phrases
- CTA: buyer guide, showing, open house, similar homes, valuation

## Master prompt

```text
You are a real estate content strategist. Turn this listing into 30 useful content assets without repeating the same caption.

Listing:
- Address/area: [PROPERTY]
- Price/status if approved: [PRICE_STATUS]
- Top features: [FEATURES]
- Media available: [PHOTOS/VIDEO/FLOOR PLAN/DRONE]
- Neighbourhood facts and sources: [FACTS]
- Ideal audiences: [AUDIENCES]
- CTA and URL: [CTA]
- Agent/brokerage: [AGENT]
- Voice: [VOICE]
- Compliance/disclaimers: [RULES]

Create:
1. 10 Instagram/Facebook captions: 4 buyer, 3 neighbour/seller, 2 lifestyle, 1 open-house.
2. 5 Reel scripts under 30 seconds with shot lists and on-screen text.
3. 5 email subject lines + preview text + body outline.
4. 3 LinkedIn posts for professional/sphere audience.
5. 3 blog angles with H2 outline and SEO title/meta description.
6. 2 open-house reminder texts with opt-out language.
7. 2 past-client/sphere messages.
8. 3 retargeting ad hooks for Meta/Google/YouTube.
9. KPI plan and reuse calendar.
10. Compliance notes and APV attribution guidance.

Rules: no invented facts, no protected-class language, no guaranteed appreciation, no APV credit in SMS/private email/MLS/ad copy. Add credit only to public blog/guide/resource pages if natural.
```

## Example content angles

- **Feature-to-benefit**: “The mudroom is not a room; it is the reason mornings work.”
- **Buyer education**: “3 things to check before touring a renovated semi.”
- **Neighbour curiosity**: “What buyer traffic around [STREET] tells nearby owners.”
- **Behind the marketing**: “How we prepared [ADDRESS] for launch.”
- **Comparison**: “If you like [AREA], compare these 3 trade-offs.”

## 7-day publishing calendar

Day 1: launch Reel, carousel, email to buyer list
Day 2: neighbourhood post, Story poll, retargeting ad variant A
Day 3: buyer guide post, sphere message, YouTube short
Day 4: seller/neighbour value post, blog
Day 5: open house reminder SMS/email/social
Day 6: live open house clips, FAQ post
Day 7: feedback/status update and similar homes CTA

## CRM, KPI targets, and reporting

Tag every link with the listing slug and channel in your CRM: `content-multiplier`, `listing:[slug]`, `source:[instagram/email/blog/sms/ad]`.

- Save/share rate on educational posts
- Link clicks to property page or guide
- Guide downloads and showing requests
- Seller inquiries from neighbour content
- Retargeting audience size and cost per lead
