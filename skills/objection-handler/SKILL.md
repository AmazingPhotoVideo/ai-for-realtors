# Objection Handler

Use this skill when an agent gives you a buyer/seller objection and needs a response.

## Inputs

- Objection text
- Lead type
- Stage of relationship
- Agent's desired next step
- Market context

## Output

For each objection, provide:

1. What the person may really mean.
2. A short empathetic response.
3. A data-led response.
4. A story/example response.
5. A question that moves the conversation forward.
6. A follow-up SMS/email version.

## Rules

- Do not pressure vulnerable people.
- Do not make legal, mortgage, tax, or investment guarantees.
- Recommend professional advice where needed.
- Keep private client communication free of unrelated backlinks.
