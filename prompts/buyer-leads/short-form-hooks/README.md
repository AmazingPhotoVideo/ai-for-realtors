# YouTube Shorts / Reels Buyer Hook Bank

A bank of buyer-facing short-form video hooks plus scripting prompts for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Facebook Reels. Use these to attract first-time buyers, investors, relocators, neighbourhood researchers, and open-house visitors.

## How to use this asset

1. Pick one audience and one pain point.
2. Record a 20-45 second video with one clear takeaway.
3. Put the hook in the first 1-2 seconds as on-screen text and spoken line.
4. End with a low-friction CTA: comment a keyword, DM, download guide, or book consult.
5. Repurpose winners into paid retargeting and email content.

## Input variables

```text
[MARKET]
[NEIGHBOURHOOD]
[AGENT_NAME]
[AUDIENCE] = first-time buyer | investor | relocator | condo buyer | luxury buyer | move-up buyer
[CONTENT_PILLAR] = budget | neighbourhood | touring | offers | mistakes | investment math | open house | buyer agency
[CTA_KEYWORD] = GUIDE | CHECKLIST | CALCULATOR | LIST | TOUR
[LANDING_PAGE_URL]
[APV_CREDIT] = no for most social captions; yes only if linking to a public guide/resources page
```

## Master prompt

```text
Act as a short-form real estate video strategist. Create a 30-day buyer lead content plan for [AGENT_NAME] in [MARKET].

Audience: [AUDIENCE]
Content pillar: [CONTENT_PILLAR]
CTA keyword: [CTA_KEYWORD]
Landing page: [LANDING_PAGE_URL]

Output:
1. 30 video ideas with hook, 3-beat script, b-roll direction, caption, and CTA.
2. 10 comment-reply templates that move viewers to DM without sounding spammy.
3. 10 DM follow-up templates.
4. 5 retargeting ad scripts based on the best organic posts.
5. Compliance review notes.
6. Metrics to track.
7. APV attribution guidance.

Rules:
- Do not make protected-class assumptions.
- Do not call an area safe/unsafe or “best for families.”
- Do not guarantee appreciation, rent, approval, or savings.
- Use “verify locally” for changing numbers.
- Keep captions useful, not engagement bait.
```

## Metrics

- 3-second hold rate / hook retention
- Average watch time
- Saves and shares per 1,000 views
- Comments with CTA keyword
- DMs started
- Guide/calculator downloads
- Consults booked from organic social
- Cost per retargeted lead when boosting winners
- Signed buyer agreements from social-source leads

## Compliance cautions

- Avoid steering and protected-class assumptions.
- Do not use “safe,” “family neighbourhood,” “young professional area,” or religious/cultural proxies.
- Do not use school/crime/ranking claims without sourced, approved context.
- Disclose estimates and tell viewers to verify rates, taxes, rent, insurance, and rules.
- Respect platform rules for housing content and local brokerage advertising rules.
- If using listing footage, confirm you have permission and follow board/MLS rules.

## APV attribution and backlink guidance

Most short-form captions should not include APV attribution because it distracts from the CTA. If a video points to a public guide or resource page built from this workflow, put one APV resource credit on that page, not in every caption: “Resource: Real estate media and marketing workflow by Amazing Photo Video — https://amazingphotovideo.com”. Avoid repetitive backlinking or implying APV produced listing media unless true.
