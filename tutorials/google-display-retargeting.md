# Google Display Retargeting

Google Display keeps your listing, buyer guide, or valuation offer visible across websites and apps after someone visits your page.

## Best fit

- Property-page visitors who did not inquire
- Market report or valuation page visitors
- Open-house packet downloaders
- Seller/farm audiences with first-party consent

## Policy baseline

Google restricts targeting for housing ads in the U.S. and Canada, including certain demographic and ZIP-code uses. Keep housing campaigns compliant, avoid sensitive or discriminatory targeting, and review current Google Ads policy before launch.

## Setup checklist

1. Install Google tag/GA4 and link Google Ads.
2. Create conversions: form submit, call click, schedule click, valuation request.
3. Build audience segments: property visitors, guide downloaders, valuation visitors, open-house visitors.
4. Add UTMs: `utm_source=google&utm_medium=display&utm_campaign=[asset]`.
5. Create responsive display ads with multiple headlines, descriptions, logos, and images.
6. Exclude converted leads where appropriate.
7. Start small: $5–$25/day per funnel; increase only when conversion tracking is clean.

## Audience windows

- 1–7 days: hot reminder, showing/open house CTA
- 8–30 days: similar homes, buyer guide, market report
- 31–90 days: brand/seller nurture if compliant and enough volume

## Responsive display ad copy

Headlines:
- See the full guide for [ADDRESS]
- Still comparing homes in [AREA]?
- Get similar homes before the weekend
- What does buyer demand mean for your home?
- Request a local value range

Descriptions:
- Photos, floor plan notes, showing options, and local context in one quick guide.
- If this home is close but not perfect, get a shortlist of similar options.
- Nearby owners can compare their home against current buyer activity and recent sales.

CTA: Learn More / Get Guide / Request Info

## Banner concepts

1. Hero exterior + “Full property guide”
2. Kitchen/living detail + “See photos + floor plan”
3. Map/neighbourhood image + “Homes like this in [AREA]”
4. Seller angle + “Curious what your home could sell for?”

## Optimization

- Exclude poor placements and mobile apps if quality is weak.
- Cap frequency if impressions climb without clicks.
- Separate buyer and seller CTA ad groups.
- Compare view-through conversions cautiously; prioritize CRM-attributed appointments.
- Refresh images every 10–14 days for active campaigns.

## KPIs

- Cost per engaged landing-page visit
- View-through and click-through conversions, separated
- Assisted conversions in GA4/CRM
- Lead-to-conversation and appointment rate
- Placement quality and frequency
