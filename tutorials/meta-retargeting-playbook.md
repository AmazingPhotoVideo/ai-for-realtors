# Meta Retargeting Playbook

Use Meta to stay visible to warm listing traffic on Facebook and Instagram while respecting housing-ad rules.

## What to build

- Website audience: property page visitors 1–30 days
- Engagement audience: Instagram/Facebook listing video engagers 1–30 days
- Lead audience: opted-in CRM contacts, uploaded only with proper consent
- Exclusions: submitted lead, booked showing, seller client if appropriate

## Compliance baseline

Real estate ads belong in Meta’s Housing Special Ad Category when promoting listings, housing opportunities, or real estate services. Expect restricted targeting. Avoid age/gender/protected-class filters and discriminatory language.

## Setup checklist

1. Verify domain in Meta Business Manager.
2. Install Pixel and Conversions API if possible.
3. Configure events: ViewContent, Lead, Contact, Schedule.
4. Add UTMs to every ad: `utm_source=meta&utm_medium=retargeting&utm_campaign=[listing]`.
5. Create custom audiences from site visits and video engagement.
6. Set campaign category to Housing.
7. Launch with simple budget: $5–$20/day per active listing/farm depending on traffic.
8. Review every 3–4 days; optimize creative before over-tightening audiences.

## Campaign structure

Campaign: `[Listing] Warm Retargeting`

Ad set 1: property page visitors 30 days
Ad set 2: IG/FB listing engagers 30 days
Ad set 3: opted-in CRM list if allowed

Placements: Advantage+ placements unless creative quality suffers. Use square, vertical, and story-safe versions.

## Creative angles

### Buyer reminder

Primary text:

> Still thinking about homes in [AREA]? [ADDRESS] has the layout, location, and details buyers are asking about. See photos, showing times, and the buyer guide here.

Headline: View the property guide

CTA: Learn More

### Similar homes

> If [ADDRESS] is close but not perfect, I can send similar homes in [AREA] before your weekend tours.

Headline: Get similar homes

### Neighbour/seller

> A listing near you is creating real buyer activity. If you own in [AREA], get a quick read on how current demand may affect your home’s value.

Headline: Request a local value range

## Creative specs

- 9:16 Reel/Story: 6–15 seconds, captions burned in
- 1:1 feed: hero exterior + 3 feature callouts
- Carousel: exterior, kitchen/living, primary, outdoor, CTA card
- Avoid overlays that imply exclusivity or urgency unless true

## Follow-up flow

Lead form or landing-page form → instant SMS/email → CRM task due in 5 minutes → day-1 question → day-3 similar listing/value CTA → day-7 market update.

## KPIs

- Frequency: keep under fatigue; refresh creative when CTR drops or comments go stale
- CTR: compare against your own account benchmarks
- Cost per landing-page view and cost per lead by listing
- Lead-to-conversation rate
- Showing/valuation appointments, not just leads

## Troubleshooting

- Low audience size: combine page visitors with video engagers and extend to 60–90 days where appropriate.
- High clicks/no leads: page too slow, weak CTA, form too long, or no instant value.
- Ad rejection: verify Housing category, remove restricted targeting/language, simplify claims.
- Poor quality leads: switch from instant form to landing page or add intent question.
