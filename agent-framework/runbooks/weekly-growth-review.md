# Weekly Growth Review Runbook

Run this weekly to turn CRM, content, social metrics, and local conversations into a practical growth plan.

## Inputs

- CRM lead source counts and conversions
- pipeline changes
- tasks completed/missed
- email/SMS response rates if available
- social metrics and top content
- open-house and listing activity
- local/farm notes
- user corrections and preferences from the week

## Review sections

### 1. Pipeline reality

- new leads by source
- booked appointments
- signed clients/listings
- stuck opportunities
- oldest hot lead without next step
- source quality notes

### 2. Follow-up quality

- leads without first response
- leads without next task
- overdue tasks by category
- unanswered client/prospect emails
- nurture campaigns that need fresh content

### 3. Content performance

- top 3 posts by reach/engagement/business signal
- posts that produced DMs, profile visits, saves, shares, or clicks
- underperforming formats to stop or revise
- questions/comments that should become content

### 4. Farm-area opportunities

- local questions buyers/sellers are asking
- community events or changes
- listing activity themes
- seller objections noticed this week
- one direct-mail/email/social idea

### 5. Next week's plan

Produce:

- 3 revenue priorities
- 5 content ideas
- 1 CRM cleanup action
- 1 prospecting block recommendation
- 1 experiment to measure
