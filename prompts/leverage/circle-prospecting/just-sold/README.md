# Just-Sold Equity Outreach Kit

Use a completed sale to create useful homeowner conversations: pricing context, buyer spillover, and quiet listing opportunities.

## Best use cases

- Sale is firm/closed and public claims are allowed by local rules.
- You can cite accurate list price, sale price, days on market, offer count, or pricing strategy.
- Nearby owners have relevant comparables but need interpretation, not hype.

## Required inputs

- Sold property facts: address/area, property type, list price, sale price if publishable, DOM, offer scenario if publishable
- Comparable area: streets/building/condo stack/farm radius
- Agent role: listing agent, buyer agent, or market commentator
- CTA: instant value range, 10-minute pricing call, market report, buyer spillover list
- Required disclaimers and solicitation rules

## Field-ready copy

### Call script

> Hi [NAME], it’s [AGENT] with [BROKERAGE]. I’m calling because [ADDRESS/AREA] just sold, and it is one of the better recent indicators for homes near you. I’m not assuming you are moving — I’m asking whether you want the two-minute version of what that sale suggests for values on [STREET/BUILDING].

If yes:

> Great. The key variables were [FACT 1], [FACT 2], and [FACT 3]. If you send me your address, I can compare your home against it and send a realistic range, not an automated estimate.

### Door-knock script

> Hi, I’m [AGENT]. I worked on/track the sale at [ADDRESS/AREA]. I’m giving nearby owners a short breakdown because one sale rarely tells the whole story. The QR code has the sale summary, comparable notes, and a private value-check request if you want your home compared properly.

### SMS

> Hi [NAME], [ADDRESS/AREA] just sold and it may affect nearby values. Want the quick breakdown for [STREET/BUILDING]? I can send the summary. Reply STOP to opt out.

### Email

Subject: What [ADDRESS/AREA] means for nearby values

Hi [NAME],

[ADDRESS/AREA] has sold. The headline number matters less than the context:

- Property type/condition: [FACT]
- Pricing strategy: [FACT]
- Buyer response: [FACT]
- Closest relevant comparison: [FACT]

If you want, I can compare your home against this sale and send a realistic value range using recent local data.

[CTA]

[AGENT]

### Postcard

Front: **[ADDRESS/AREA] sold. Here is what neighbours should know.**

Back:

The sale gives nearby owners a new data point, but your home’s value still depends on condition, layout, frontage, parking, updates, and timing.

Scan for the short sale breakdown or request a private value range.

[QR]

[AGENT], [BROKERAGE]

## 14-day nurture

- Day 0: call/door/postcard drop
- Day 1: send sale summary to responders
- Day 3: email/SMS “3 factors that changed the result”
- Day 7: invite to request a private valuation
- Day 14: send micro-market update: active, pending, sold, months of inventory
- Monthly: neighbourhood market note until they opt out

## CRM

Tags: `listing-leverage-just-sold`, `seller-curious`, `equity-check`, `neighbour`, `comp:[address]`.

Stages: New → Sent sale summary → Requested range → Pricing conversation → Listing consult booked → Long-term seller nurture.

## KPIs

- 100 owner touches → 15–30 conversations
- 5–12 sale-summary requests
- 2–6 valuation requests
- 1–2 listing consultations in warm farm areas

## Compliance cautions

- Confirm sale price/public reporting rules before using numbers.
- Avoid “your home is worth X” without a proper analysis and disclaimers.
- Do not imply guaranteed results or that you have buyers unless true and documented.
- Respect solicitation restrictions for homes already listed or represented.
